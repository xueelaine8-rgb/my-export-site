Product Introduction

Fiberglass chopped strand mat is a non-woven reinforcement material made from randomly distributed chopped glass fibers bonded together with powder or emulsion binder.

It is widely used in FRP products, tanks, pipes, boat hulls, automotive parts, and construction applications.

Core Advantages

High Wet-Out

Fast resin penetration improves production efficiency.

Isotropic Strength

Uniform fiber distribution provides balanced strength.

Corrosion Resistance

Excellent resistance to acid and alkali environments.
